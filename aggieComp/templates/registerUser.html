How to Register...

We do not want users to forget their username so we recommend that user register with their nmsu email. For example, "johnsmith@nmsu.edu" would work. However, any email works.


Upon registeration, should the email entered not already be register, our system will create an account with the first part of the email. For example, an account registered with the email "johnsmith@nmsu.edu" will have an account created with the username "johnsmith". This username is used when logging in.
