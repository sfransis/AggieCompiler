What is CSisEz?

We are an attempt at making it easier than ever to learn about computer science topics as well as implement them.

Our group has found that the information and resources provided to CS students at NMSU keeps those students on a relatively linear path. This is fine as one is provided with the opportunity to learn the fundamentals of cs as well as apply them albeit in a limited way. This does not however foster a desire to learn more than what is shown.
